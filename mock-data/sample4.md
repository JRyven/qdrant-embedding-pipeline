The sun folds itself over the field like a warm linen sheet.
Heat shimmers off the blacktop, and somewhere a ribbon of wind moves
the tall grass in slow, concordant waves — a quiet tide of green.

Children's laughter is a small, bright weather; a neighbor mows;
the sky is big and patient, and clouds drift like lazy sails.
No boats today, only the distant gull— a sound of sea memory.

Shared words: sun, wind, tide, drift, calm, heat, foam.
