---
title: Mirror Poem - Spring on the Floating House
date: 2025-04-10
---

I. On the dock: the spring wind comes sharp —
   spray, then lull; the harbor answers back.

II. On the hull: the hull answers back —
   lull, then spray; the sharp wind comes spring on.

The poem mirrors itself: movement and counter-movement, storm and then calm.
Boat-words: hull, mast, stern, bow, harbor, foam, storm.

Shared words: boat, storm, harbor, foam, mast, wind, tide.
